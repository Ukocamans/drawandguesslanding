1. Introduction

Draw & Guess ("the App") is a mobile drawing puzzle game developed for iOS. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our App. By using the App, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

2. Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information:

a) Analytics Data

We collect anonymous usage data to improve the App experience, including:

  • Device type and operating system version
  • App usage patterns (screens visited, features used, level progress, session and total gameplay duration)
  • Crash reports, events and performance data
  • General geographic region (country/city level, not precise location)
  • Language preference for the App

b) Advertising Data

Our App displays advertisements provided by third-party ad networks. These networks may collect:

  • Advertising identifier (IDFA, subject to your consent via App Tracking Transparency)
  • Device information and IP address
  • Ad interaction data (views, clicks)

c) Locally Stored Data

Game progress, settings, in app purchases and preferences are stored locally on your device using UserDefaults. This data is not transmitted to any server except in app purchases. In such case in app purchases are managed through app store procedures to retrieve purchases if needed.

3. How We Use Your Information

Legal basis for personal data processing:

  • When the use of your data is required to execute our obligations under a contract with you
  • When the use of your data is necessary for our legitimate interests or the legitimate interests of others
  • When the use of your information is needed to comply with legal obligations or exercise rights

We use the collected information to:

  • Analyze app performance and improve user experience
  • Display relevant advertisements if the user consents
  • Fix bugs and improve app stability
  • Understand general usage trends

4. Third-Party Services

The App integrates the following third-party services that may collect data:

Google AdMob: For displaying advertisements. Google's privacy policy

Firebase Analytics: For anonymous usage analytics. Firebase privacy

These third parties have their own privacy policies governing data they collect.

5. Advertising & App Tracking Transparency

On iOS 14.5 and later, we request your permission via Apple's App Tracking Transparency (ATT) framework before collecting your advertising identifier (IDFA). You may deny this request, and the App will continue to function normally. You can change this setting at any time in your device's Settings > Privacy > Tracking.

6. Children's Privacy

Our App is not explicitly aimed at children. If you are a user under the certain age based on the applicable legislation and using a third-party account, your parents must allow you to use our services.

7. Data Retention

Analytics data is retained in aggregated, anonymized form. We do not store personally identifiable information on our servers. Local game data remains on your device until you delete the App.

8. Data Security

Although we strive to protect your data and privacy, no security method or firewall can be guaranteed to protect information from hackers or human error. Consequently, we expressly disclaim responsibility or liability for the loss, theft, destruction, or inadvertent disclosure of your data.

9. Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have the right to:

  • Access the personal data collected about you, if it exists
  • Request deletion of your personal data, if it exists
  • Opt out of personalized advertising
  • Withdraw consent for tracking

To exercise these rights, contact us at support@doodleandguess.com.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted within the App and the effective date will be updated. Continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

11. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at: support@doodleandguess.com
